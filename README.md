Key Insights
Sales showed steady growth over time.
Technology category performed best in sales.
Some categories had high sales but lower profit.
Regional differences significantly impacted performance.
Higher discounts often reduced profit.
Recommendations
Focus on high-performing categories.
Improve discount strategy to protect profit.
Retain top customers with targeted strategies.
Work on improving low-profit segments.
